Quick Start Guide

1

Open Home Page

Click "Home" above or open index.html to see the landing page with features, plans, and BMI calculator.

2

Register New Account

Click "Sign Up" and follow the 4-step registration process. Fill your personal info, fitness profile, choose a plan, and complete payment.

3

Login to Dashboard

Use your credentials to login. You'll be redirected to your personal dashboard with stats, workout plans, and progress charts.

4

Configure Firebase (Optional)

For persistent data, follow the SETUP_GUIDE.html to integrate Firebase, Razorpay, and email services.

5

Deploy to Production

Use Firebase Hosting or Vercel to deploy your app. Check README.md for detailed deployment instructions.
